FAQ: Company Policies

Q: How do I request remote work?
A: Submit a request to your manager stating the days you want to work remotely. Approval is required.

Q: When are expenses due?
A: Expense reports must be submitted within 30 days of the purchase date.

Q: Are receipts mandatory?
A: Receipts are required for expenses above $25.

Q: What should I do if I lack a receipt?
A: If you lack a receipt, the expense may be denied or partially reimbursed at manager discretion.
